About Pindah

Pindah Private Limited is a Zimbabwe-based enterprise software company founded in 2024. We build practical, localized software that helps schools and businesses digitize operations, improve decision-making, and scale sustainably in African markets.

Our approach is simple: enterprise software should adapt to organizations, not force organizations to adapt to software. We focus on affordability, compliance, localization, and implementation simplicity.
